This repository contains a **Power BI Sales Dashboard** built using the **Global Superstore** dataset. The dashboard provides a clear, interactive overview of sales performance, profitability, category contribution, regional performance, and top-selling products.

## Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI Desktop**
* **DAX for calculated measures**

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Displayed at the top of the dashboard:

* **Total Sales:** 338,256.97
* **Total Profit:** 32,950.75
* **Profit Margin:** 9.74%

All KPIs are dynamic and respond to slicer selections.

## Visualizations Included

### 1. Category Breakdown (Bar Chart)

* Compares sales across product categories:

  * Technology
  * Office Supplies
  * Furniture
    
* **Technology** is the highest-performing category (~0.13M in sales).

### 2. Sales Over Time (Line Chart)

* Displays total sales trends across the selected date range.
* Helps identify fluctuations and overall movement in sales performance.

### 3. Region Performance (Bar Chart)

* Ranks regions based on total sales contribution.
* **Central** region leads with approximately 71K in sales, followed by **South** and **North**.
* Some regions (e.g., Canada) show minimal contribution.

### 4. Top 10 Products (Table)

* Lists the top 10 products by total sales.
* Supports quick identification of high-revenue products.
